ConcordRENTS is a national leader in providing high-quality, affordable, multifamily housing and understands the importance of a strong community and the value of giving back. With the affordable housing crisis in Orlando, FL and the rest of Central Florida, we understand the struggle in finding quality affordable housing.
Below is a list of our CURRENT Available Affordable Housing Inventory around Central Florida. Apartment homes are income restricted and are subject to Maximum Allowable Household Incomes listed for each community location. As part of the application process, applicants must submit the verification documentation in a timely manner, as the first approved and full deposit paid applicant will have priority of the available apartment home.
